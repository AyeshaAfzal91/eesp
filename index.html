EESP Workshop 2025

Energy Efficiency with Sustainable Performance

In conjunction with ISC High Performance 2025

About the Workshop

The EESP workshop focuses on innovative strategies to enhance energy efficiency in computing environments, providing insights into greener, cost-effective practices for exascale and Tier-1/2 centers.

Important Dates

  • Submission Deadline: February 20, 2025 (extended: March 1, 2025)
  • Notification: March 28, 2025
  • Camera-Ready Deadline: April 30, 2025
  • Workshop: June 10, 2025

Call for Papers

We invite academia, supercomputing centers, and industry to submit papers on energy-efficient computing practices. Topics include:

  • Energy-aware software optimization
  • Energy-efficient hardware practices
  • Energy-efficient scheduling and resource management
  • Renewable energy sources for HPC systems
